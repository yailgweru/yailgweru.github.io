Launching the Gweru Hub

1 March 2026 · Hub Team
#launch#gweru#ai-for-good#community

When the Young AI Leaders Community opened its call for hubs, a small group of us in Gweru saw an opening. The global AI conversation was happening everywhere except here — so we applied, we pitched, and the Midlands got its seat at the table.

A simple promise

The hub launched with a simple promise: AI in Gweru should be built by Gweru. Our first cohort brought together students, teachers, developers and farmers' children — different reasons for showing up, one shared question: what can this technology do for us?

Since then the hub has grown into a community of young leaders running workshops, building prototypes and representing Zimbabwe in the global AI for Good network under the ITU.

What's next

This blog is where we tell those stories — the wins, the failed experiments and everything between. Welcome in.
